OurHistory

Like any other unique place, we have our own special story. The idea for the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. During a walk in the forest, the creator of our restaurant found themselves hundreds of kilometers away from the nearest settlement. Far from civilization and without any connection, they had to temporarily set up a simple way of life, sourcing and preparing food on their own.
